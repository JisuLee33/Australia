GOLD COAST

where golden white sand and winding waves run for two kilometers.

CAIRNS

The relaxed city of Cairns boasts an ideal location as a starting point for a trip to explore the Great Barrier Reef and the Daintree rainforest,
two of the fantastic landmarks on the World Heritage List.
Whether you're travelling across islands or planning to travel through rainforests, you can experience the unique wildlife,
luxurious resorts and rich native cultures of this charming area.
You can't say you traveled to Cairns without diving or snorkeling in the Great Barrier Reef.
